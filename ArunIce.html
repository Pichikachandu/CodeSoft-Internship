About Us

Arun IceCreams and It's a brand not like ordinary ice creams

Arun Icecreams was started by Mr. R.G. Chandramogan in 1970. In 1985, the brand topped the ice cream sales in terms of volume list in Tamil Nadu. By 1999, around 700 outlets were present in Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Kerala, and Andhra Pradesh, and 2,300 parlors as of 2018. The brand extended to Maharashtra and Orissa. Our motto is to provide high-quality ice creams nationwide.
